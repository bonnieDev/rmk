Where does the rain go?

Water is always on the move. The sun warms puddles, rivers, and the sea until the water turns into an invisible mist that floats up into the sky. High up where it is cold, the mist gathers into clouds — and when a cloud grows heavy enough, the water falls back down as rain. Then the whole trip begins again.
